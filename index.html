What is SDX?

SDX is an advanced form of modding for 7 Days to Die. Add new assets, new blocks, new animations, and completely new functionality.

Why use SDX over traditional XML mod?

Take the game to new heights and limits through using SDX:

  • Add new custom models
  • Add new sounds
  • Add the Bigger Back Pack Mod
7D2D SDX: Expand the game to your imagination.
